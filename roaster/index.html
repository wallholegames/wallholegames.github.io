Roaster simulator game

My Roaster is a chicken roaster simulator game. Roast chickens and sell them to your customers. Don't relax! Your customers become more and more anxious, you have to be very fast roasting chickens.

Roaster screensaver

After a hard day, you can relax with the screensaver mode. Turn your device into a virtual roaster. Place some chickens in the roaster, and enjoy a relaxing view of them being cooked in real-time, with a background crackling sound.

Unlock extras

Each sale gives you in-game coins, and you can use these coins to unlock improvements that help to maintain your business longer. Earn chicken-coins selling chickens in the game. Unlock new kinds of chickens with your chicken-coins.
