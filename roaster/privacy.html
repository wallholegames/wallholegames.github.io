Privacy policy

The Privacy Policy establishes the conditions of use of My Roaster app. The use of My Roaster implies the acceptance of these conditions. We reserve the right to modify the Privacy Policy at any time.

Data protection

Wallhole Games does not collect any kind of user data from My Roaster. The application does not ask for personal information. It may share game statistics and scores through certain third party services: Game Center and Google Play Games, if these services are configured on the device, and Facebook prior user authorization.

Advertisement

My Roaster shows Admob ads. For more information, check Admob Privacy Policy.

Social

My Roaster may share score and achievements through Game Center in iOS (see Game Center Terms and Conditions), and through Google Play Games in Android (see Google Privacy Policy).

My Roaster includes a tool to share information on Facebook. The publication on Facebook must be previously authorized by the user. The application only includes information about game statistics and score.
